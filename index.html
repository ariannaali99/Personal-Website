How I ended up studying Computer Science

In my final years of high school, most of my courses were Mathematics-based. However, I wasn't too fond of the idea of getting a BSc in Mathematics. I was really worried about choosing my major for university when one of my professor's recommeneded that I try Computer Science. At the time, I didn't even know how to code but I wasn't sure what else to do. However, as I started taking classes, I realized that it wasn't scary at all and most people in my major had no prior programming experience. I actually grew to enjoy programming as the process is somewhat similar to solving problems in Mathematics.
